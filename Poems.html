Something special for you...

A Girlfriends promise...

I promise to lift you up when you feel down.
I promise to wipe your tears when you cry.
I promise to make you happy.
I promise to be your strength when you feel weak.
I promise to be tour voice when you cant find the words.


I promise to be your smile when you're frowning.
I promise to always be honest and tell you the truth.
I promise to cheer you up when you are sad and blue.
I promise to give you confidence when you feel insecure.


I promise to listen whenever you need to talk.
I promise to be your shoulder to cry on.
I promise to always hold you whenever you need someone.


I promise to always care for you.
I promise to support you through thick and thin.
I promise to never hurt you and break your heart.
I cant promise you the world, the sky, the sun. moon, and stars.
I cant promise you that we wont fight.
I cant promise you that it will always be a happy relationship
But i can promise you that i can always be true to you, love you and care for you till end of time.

-Shavina Pillay
